Meet Sharma ji. He is 72. He lives alone in Delhi. Every morning he stares at 6 medicine bottles. He can't read the labels anymore. Last month, he took his diabetes pill twice and skipped BP medicine for 3 days. He was hospitalized.

A letter came — Ayushman Bharat, ₹5 lakh free health coverage approved. He couldn't understand the document. He never claimed it.

His son calls every Sunday. "Sab theek hai, beta," he says. But he forgot his granddaughter's name last week.

India has 140 million Sharma jis. Invisible. Forgotten. Until now.
